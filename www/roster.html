Roster Jobs

Families are asked to complete two maintenance duties per semester (one per term). A Maintance Levy is charged in Semester 1 on the Term 1 account. If maintenace duties are completed, this money is carried forward to Semester 2 and refunded at the end of the year.

Maintenance Days are organised by the Maintenance Officer and run from 8:00am to 12:00pm. Duties on the day might include gardening, painting, mending of equipment and cleaning. If you are unable to attend any of the nominated Maintenance Days you may be able to arrange to complete some other tasks (eg Sewing, Library Repairs, Washing).
