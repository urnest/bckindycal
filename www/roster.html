Roster Jobs

Families are asked to complete two maintenance duties per semester (one per term). A Maintance Levy is charged in Semester 1 on the Term 1 account. If maintenace duties are completed, this money is carried forward to Semester 2 and refunded at the end of the year.

There are 3 types of roster jobs:

  • Class Jobs are allocated per class.
  • Kindy-Wide Jobs apply to the whole center. Volunteers can be from any class.
  • Maintenance Days etc apply to the whole centre on a particular date. Maintenance Days usually run from 8:00am to 12:00pm. Duties might include gardening, painting, mending of equipment and cleaning.
